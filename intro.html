Regional Meals

Growing up, my family and I got to travel to a number of places in Nigeria. As such, I got to taste some lovely meals that were perculiar to certain regions of Nigeria.

So here are some regional meals for us to explore.

Northern Nigeria

  1. Tuwo shinkafa with miyan kuka
  2. Fura de nunu
  3. Masa with miyan kubewa

Southern Nigeria

  1. Pounded yam and banga soup
  2. Sea food plantain porridge
  3. Abacha

There're so much more meals within these regions. Noentheless, the aim is to share about my experiences in both cooking and eating these amazing meals.:)
